AIG Travel Guard Platinum

    AIG Travel Guard is the largest travel insurance provider in the United States, protecting more than 6 million travelers every year.
  • Broadest coverage in the travel insurance industry
  • Claims reviewed within 24 hours of receipt
  • Based in Stevens Point, WI
    We offer the following plans from AIG Travel Guard:
  • Basic (a comprehensive plan good for budget-savvy travelers)
  • Silver (another budget-friendly plan, offering free coverage for one child per covered adult)
  • Gold (good for families with multiple children; all children under 17 are covered for free with one covered adult)
  • Platinum (offers the most comprehensive coverage with the greatest number of options)
  • MedEvac (emergency medical evacuation coverage for single trips)
  • Flight Guard (offers multiple levels of coverage for Accidental Death and Dismemberment)
  • Gold Deluxe (coverage options for residents of Canada)
