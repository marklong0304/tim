AIG Travel Guard Platinum

The Platinum plan offers the highest coverage limits of the comprehensive trip cost protection plans offered by AIG Travel Guard, and as with the other plans, it is best to purchase soon after making your initial payment so that you receive the most benefits. This plan offers some time sensitive coverages, meaning that you have to purchase the plan within 15 days of your initial trip deposit to be eligible for those coverages to be available.

    Time sensitive benefits are:
  • Financial Default
  • Optional Cancel for Any Reason (view full plan details for eligibility requirements)
  • Pre-Existing Conditions Waiver (view full plan details for eligibility requirements)
    Coverages included within this plan are:
  • Trip Cancellation and Interruption for a covered reason
  • Baggage Coverage
  • Travel Delay
  • Medical
  • Emergency Medical Evacuation
  • Accidental Death & Dismemberment

In addition to insurance coverage, there are also assistance services that may be helpful prior to and during your trip. Whether you need travel information, including visa or passport information, or a medical referral while on your trip, you are able to access these services 24/7.
